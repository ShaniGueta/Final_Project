Informed Consent Form for Experimental Participation

Title of Study: Decision-Making Experiment

You are invited to participate in a research experiment conducted online using the Prolific platform.
Before you decide whether to participate, it is important for you to understand the nature of the study,
what will be expected of you, and any potential risks or benefits. Please read this informed consent form carefully.

Purpose of the Study: The purpose of this study is to investigate decision-making processes in group settings when individuals have access to partial information.

Procedures: You may be asked to provide some personal information, including your email, age, and gender.
You will receive instructions about the experiment's course, including how decisions are made in various scenarios.
You will engage in decision-making tasks as described in the experiment's scenarios.

Risks and Benefits: There are no known physical risks associated with this study.
However, as with any online activity, there may be potential risks related to data security and privacy,
but we do not collect any personal user data and you are not recorded at any time during the experiment. We will make every effort to protect your information.

The potential benefits of participating include contributing to scientific research in the field of decision-making and the opportunity to earn financial rewards based on your decisions during the experiment.

For completing the experiment, you will get 416 clickcoins, a mockup currency equivalent to $1.25 (based on the exchange rate of 1 ClickCoin = $0.003) your performance may result in either an increase or decrease in this amount.

Confidentiality: Your personal information, including email,
will not be recorded (other than your prolific ID, for payment purposes).
Data collected during the experiment will be anonymized and aggregated for analysis.

Publishing the Results: The results of the experiment will be used to expand scientific knowledge on the subject being studied and may be described in publications,
such as academic conferences, articles, or research papers.

Voluntary Participation: Participation in this study is entirely voluntary.
You have the right to withdraw from the experiment, but in this case, you will not get any payment.

Payment Method: You will get the payment approximately within a week.

Contact Information: If you have any questions or concerns about this study, please contact us through one of the following emails:
barhot@post.bgu.ac.il, galeta@post.bgu.ac.il, guets@post.bgu.ac.il

Consent:

By clicking the "I Agree" button below,
you indicate that you have read and understood the information provided in this informed consent form,
and you voluntarily agree to participate in the study.
