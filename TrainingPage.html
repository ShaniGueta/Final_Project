Training Question

You have a pitcher containing balls in two colors: yellow and green.

You are asked to determine which color of ball is more common in the pitcher.

The pitcher holds a total of 100 balls, with 65 of one color and 35 of another color.

You have drawn one ball from the pitcher, and it is green.

Based on this information,
what is your choice regarding the dominant color in the pitcher?

Remember, you are making a guess based on limited information. There is no certainty in your answer.
